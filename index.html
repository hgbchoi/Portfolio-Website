Nice to Meet You!

I aspire to become a full stack developer who knows how to tackle challenging problems and writes efficient code that everyone can understand.

Who Am I?

I am a recent systems engineering graduate of Simon Fraser University. Throughout my studies I learned how to collaborate with teammates, breakdown complex problems into manageable pieces, and not to give up when the going gets tough. I am constantly learning each day, developing my skills to reach my goal.

What Do I Like About Software Development?

The idea of creating something useful that can be enjoyed by anyone across the globe with just a computer is absolutely astonishing to me. Throughout my internship as a quality assurance analyst, I gained first hand experience of the agile software development life cycle. The challenges that developers faced in the company each day were those that I thought I would love to be able to solve someday and this motivated me to start learning the neccessary technologies.

Things I'm Learning

Since now that I have a basic working knowledge of the main languages used for front-end web development, I am currently focused on learning some of the server side languages such as PHP and some of the database related techonologies such as MongoDB + MySQL Server.

In order to gain familiarity with the technologies that are widely used in the market, I am also studying the Javascript library, React.

I am also constantly learning new things about the technologies listed in the Things I Know section through building new things such as this portfolio site!

Things I Want To Learn

Through the enormous advancements in technologies used in development and its capabilities, there are near endless things that I want to sink my teeth into. One of the most interesting fields for me is machine learning because it opens up a whole new field of artificial intelligence.

On the programming language side of things, I never had the opportunity to incorporate Python into my project and I want to be able to do so since it has a reputation of being a very versatile language.
